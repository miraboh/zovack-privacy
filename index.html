Zovack Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Zovack collects the following types of information:

  • Personal information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment information: Credit card details and billing address.
  • Location information: GPS data to provide accurate delivery services.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifier.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with the Zovack application.

How We Use Your Information

Zovack uses your information for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders, promotions, and updates.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our services.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Sharing of Information

We may share your information with the following parties:

  • Third-party service providers: Delivery partners, payment processors, and marketing platforms.
  • Business partners: Restaurants and grocery stores from which you place orders.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Security

Zovack takes appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

We use encryption, secure servers, and regularly update our security practices.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • You can update your personal information in the Zovack application settings.
  • You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the instructions in the emails.
  • You can request the deletion of your account by contacting our support team.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be indicated at the top.
